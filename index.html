Hello!

I am Pavanitha

I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Science at the University of Southern California, where I'm exploring applied AI through the lens of building robust, reliable, and trustworthy systems. I'm particularly interested in how machine learning models can be integrated into real-world environments focusing on system design, data-driven pipelines, and ensuring these solutions are scalable, maintainable, and dependable in practice.

My journey in computer science started during my undergrad at IIT Dharwad, where I spent a lot of time exploring different areas from building voice-based authentication systems to developing domain-specific search engines. Along the way, I also got involved in research, which led to a couple of publications. That phase really shaped how I approach problems, breaking them down, experimenting, and iterating until something meaningful comes out of it.

After graduating, I worked as a Software Engineer at ABB Innovation Centre in Bangalore for about 1.5 years. There, I built backend systems for industrial applications, worked with real-time data processing, and contributed to end-to-end solutions deployed for clients. I had the opportunity to take ownership of key components, collaborate closely within teams, and understand how systems evolve from initial design to production environments.

What I've come to enjoy most is building systems that are not just functional, but reliable and scalable—things that hold up outside of a classroom or a demo.

Right now, I'm continuing to learn and push myself further, looking for opportunities where I can contribute to real-world systems and keep growing as an engineer. This website is a collection of what I've worked on so far and what I'm building next.

Let's connect!! I'm always open to collaborating on innovative projects or discussing the future of technology.
